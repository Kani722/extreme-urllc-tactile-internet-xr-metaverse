# Extreme URLLC and Tactile Internet for XR, Holographic, and Metaverse Applications

## Overview

This repository contains the research paper **"Extreme URLLC and Tactile Internet for XR, Holographic, and Metaverse Applications."**

The paper presents a survey of emerging communication technologies required to support immersive digital applications, including:

* Extended Reality (XR)
* Holographic Communication
* Metaverse Systems
* Tactile Internet
* Extreme Ultra-Reliable Low-Latency Communication (xURLLC)

## Research Focus

The study examines:

* Communication requirements of immersive applications
* Extreme URLLC and Tactile Internet concepts
* Edge Computing architectures
* AI-based network management
* Network Slicing
* Future 6G networking technologies
* Research challenges and future directions

## Abstract

Future communication networks must support immersive digital applications such as XR, holographic communication, and metaverse platforms. These applications require ultra-low latency, high reliability, and stable communication performance. This research surveys Extreme URLLC and Tactile Internet technologies and evaluates their role in enabling next-generation immersive communication systems. The paper also discusses enabling technologies, compares existing research approaches, identifies limitations, and highlights future research opportunities.
